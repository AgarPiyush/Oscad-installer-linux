1.For voltage source use ac,dc,sine,pulse,pwl
2.For Diode use model name e.g 1N4007
3.For Resistance e.g 100,1K,1M
4.For Capacitor e.g 1uf,10nf,1pf
5.For Inductor e.g 1mh,5nh
6.For BJT- Use npn or pnp And for MOS- Use nmos or pmos
7.For RWire either put 0 or wire resistance
8.If you have any model in your circuit use proper model through model builder
